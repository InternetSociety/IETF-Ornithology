# Drone Remote ID Protocol (drip) 
* <IETFschedule meets=true>IETF124: Wed 05 Nov 2025 12:00 - Agora</IETFschedule>
* [About DRIP](https://datatracker.ietf.org/group/drip/about/) 
* Potentially of interest because: its links to aviation requirements
* Keywords: drones, safety

This working groups works at means for unmanned aircraft systems, e.g. drones,  can identify themselves remotely using existing IETF builing blocks. It tries to align with requirements from regulatory authorities, such as the International Civil Aviation Organization, the European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA), and the US Federal Aviation Administration (US FAA).

The group is at its end of its chartered activities.
