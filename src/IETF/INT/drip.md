# Drone Remote ID Protocol (drip) 
* <IETFschedule meets=false>DRIP likely does not meet at IETF125</IETFschedule>
* [About DRIP](https://datatracker.ietf.org/group/drip/about/) 
* Potentially of interest because: its links to aviation requirements
* Keywords: drones, safety

This working groups develops means for unmanned aircraft systems, e.g. drones, so that can identify themselves remotely. The work is using existing IETF building blocks. It tries to align with requirements from regulatory authorities, such as the International Civil Aviation Organization, the European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA), and the US Federal Aviation Administration (US FAA).

The group is at its end of its chartered activities.
